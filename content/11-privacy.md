# Privacy Policy

## What Is the Purpose of This Privacy Policy?

This privacy policy is **a comprehensive legal document detailing how Fascinating Dentistry collects, uses, and manages personal information.** Fascinating Dentistry operates this website as a dental directory in Australia. We structure our data handling procedures around strict compliance with the Privacy Act 1988. 

We align our operational practices with the 13 Australian Privacy Principles (APPs) established by the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC). We maintain a strict commitment to protecting user privacy across all platform interactions. By using this website, you agree to the data practices described in this document and our [terms of service](https://fascinatingdentistry.com/terms/).

This document outlines our legal obligations regarding data protection in Australia. We ensure transparent data collection, clearly defined user rights, and highly accessible contact information. We manage all user data according to the legislative requirements set forth for digital platforms operating within Australian jurisdiction.

## What Information Do We Collect?

We collect 3 distinct categories of data from website visitors. Fascinating Dentistry functions as an informational platform. We categorise data collection into personal information, usage data, and cookies.

### Personal Information

Personal information includes specific data points that identify you as an individual. We collect personal information including your name and email address. We gather this data exclusively if you voluntarily provide these details through our contact forms or newsletter subscription modules. We do not require personal information to browse the core directory.

### Usage Data

Usage data comprises technical information transmitted automatically by your web browser. We collect usage data including your Internet Protocol (IP) address, browser type, browser version, time zone settings, and operating system. We record the exact uniform resource locators (URLs) you navigate to during your session, along with page response times, download errors, and interaction durations. We use this technical data to analyse platform trends and administer the server infrastructure.

### Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small text files placed on your local device by a website server. We utilise cookies for site functionality and session management. We implement cookies to remember your user preferences and keep track of your navigation patterns. Users retain full control over cookie settings and can disable specific tracking technologies directly within their web browser options.

### Prohibited Health Information

Fascinating Dentistry does not collect, store, or process health information, medical records, or dental history. We do not gather sensitive information as defined by Section 6 of the Privacy Act 1988. Users must refrain from submitting personal health data or confidential medical queries through our standard contact forms.

## How Do We Use Your Information?

We use your information for 4 primary operational purposes. Fascinating Dentistry utilises collected data to maintain site functionality, perform analytics, facilitate communication, and ensure network security.

We process personal information, specifically names and email addresses, to respond directly to your inquiries and deliver requested information. We send periodic updates or newsletters exclusively if a user opts in to receive such communications.

We analyse usage data and IP addresses to monitor website performance. We evaluate this data to improve site functionality and user experience. We monitor metrics to understand how visitors interact with our content, allowing us to optimise the structural layout of our dental directory.

We use technical data to detect, prevent, and address technical issues or security vulnerabilities. We utilise server logs to identify potential malicious activities, unauthorised access attempts, or systemic errors.

Fascinating Dentistry does not sell personal data to third parties. We do not trade, lease, or rent user information to external marketing agencies or commercial entities. We exclusively use your data to operate the website and provide requested services.

## How Do We Protect Your Information?

We protect your personal information using 3 dedicated security measures. We employ data encryption, secure server infrastructure, and strict access controls.

We use Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) and Transport Layer Security (TLS) cryptographic protocols for data transmission. These protocols encrypt the data exchanged between your web browser and our server infrastructure, rendering intercepted data packets unreadable to unauthorised parties.

We host all website data on secure servers located in controlled digital environments. These hosting environments operate behind advanced hardware and software firewalls designed to mitigate external cyber threats.

We enforce rigid role-based access controls within our organisation. Only authorised administrative personnel possess elevated credentials to access backend server systems and user database modules.

We maintain strict digital hygiene protocols, yet no system is 100% secure against persistent threats. We acknowledge that despite implementing robust security measures, unknown vulnerabilities exist. We continuously update our security parameters to align with prevailing cybersecurity standards and counter emerging digital threats.

## What Are Your Rights Under Australian Privacy Law?

You have 4 primary rights regarding your personal data under the Australian Privacy Principles. We facilitate your rights to access personal data, request corrections, request deletion, and lodge formal complaints.

### Right to Access

Under APP 12, you possess the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you. We provide this access within 30 days of receiving a verified request. We deliver the requested data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.

### Right to Correction

Under APP 13, you possess the right to request the correction of inaccurate, irrelevant, out-of-date, incomplete, or misleading personal information. We evaluate correction requests promptly and update our databases to reflect accurate details. We notify other authorised entities if we share the corrected data.

### Right to Deletion

You retain the right to request the deletion of your personal information. We honour deletion requests when the data is no longer necessary for the purpose for which it was collected, or if you withdraw your consent for data processing. We permanently erase the relevant data sets from our active servers and secure backups.

### Right to Complain

You possess the right to complain about our data handling practices. We manage complaints regarding privacy breaches internally through our designated contact channels. If our internal resolution does not satisfy your concerns, you retain the right to escalate the matter to the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC). The OAIC serves as the independent statutory agency responsible for regulating privacy and freedom of information in Australia.

We establish formal agreements with our service providers to ensure they apply security measures consistent with the Privacy Act 1988. We recommend reviewing the specific privacy documentation of these external tools to understand their comprehensive data handling procedures.
