## What Clinical Expertise Does Dr Anthony Au Practise?

Dr Anthony Au practises prosthodontics and restorative dentistry in private practice in Sydney. His expertise covers 3 core areas: dental implants, fixed prosthodontics, and removable prosthodontics.

With over 20 years of clinical experience, Dr Au applies evidence-based treatment approaches to complex restorative cases. His work involves full-mouth rehabilitation, implant-supported restorations, crown and bridge procedures, and management of occlusal disorders. He operates an innovative private practice that integrates current research with clinical decision-making.

Prosthodontics is the dental specialty concerned with diagnosing, planning, and executing restorative treatments for missing or damaged teeth. Fixed prosthodontics includes crowns, bridges, and implant-supported restorations that remain permanently in the mouth. Removable prosthodontics includes complete dentures and partial dentures that patients take out for cleaning. Dr Au's specialist training equips him to manage cases requiring multidisciplinary coordination, from single-tooth implants to complete arch reconstruction.

Senior lecturers in this program supervise specialist trainees, deliver didactic content, and assess clinical competency. Dr Au contributes alongside other University of Sydney faculty members.
