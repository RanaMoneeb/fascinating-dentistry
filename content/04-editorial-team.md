# Our Editorial Team

## Who Reviews Our Content?

All clinical articles on Fascinating Dentistry undergo review by **AHPRA-registered dentists with active clinical and academic experience** before publication. The Australian Health Practitioner Regulation Agency manages the national register for dental practitioners. Evidence-based dentistry requires practitioners to integrate clinical expertise with the best available research. Our dental content reviewers in Australia apply this standard to every article.

Clinical experience provides the foundation for evaluating complex dental topics. Our reviewers assess articles covering wisdom teeth extraction, periodontal disease management, and prosthodontic treatments. Academic experience ensures reviewers understand research methodologies, statistical significance, and study design. This dual-focus methodology separates Fascinating Dentistry from anonymous content mills. Reviewers evaluate our content against the guidelines set by the Australian Dental Association. Read our comprehensive [medical review process](https://fascinatingdentistry.com/medical-review/) for specific details regarding our evaluation framework.

## What Roles Make Up the Fascinating Dentistry Editorial Team?

The Fascinating Dentistry editorial team divides into two distinct operational groups. Editorial writers draft the content by synthesising academic literature. Medical reviewers verify the clinical accuracy of the drafted content. Our team is expanding, with more reviewers joining our board to cover additional dental specialties. Each member brings specific expertise to our content production system.

Writers hold qualifications in health communication or clinical sciences. They translate complex dental terminology into accessible information for patients. Reviewers hold advanced clinical degrees and specialist registrations. They correct anatomical descriptions, treatment protocols, and pharmacological references. Access the individual bio pages below to read the specific credentials for our team members, including [Dr Anthony Au](https://fascinatingdentistry.com/editorial-team/dr-anthony-au/). ## What Is Our Dental Content Review Process?

Our dental content review process involves **a three-step system focusing on clinical accuracy, citation verification, and safety checks** before publishing. Writers submit article drafts to the medical review board. Reviewers evaluate the text against current Australian Dental Association guidelines. The three-step process ensures factual consistency across the website.

| Review Stage | Reviewer Action | Outcome |
|---|---|---|
| 1. Clinical Accuracy | Compare claims with Australian Dental Association guidelines | Ensures factual alignment with national standards |
| 2. Citation Verification | Cross-check referenced studies from PubMed and Cochrane | Prevents misrepresentation of academic literature |
| 3. Safety Check | Evaluate home care advice and treatment summaries | Protects patient health by removing hazardous recommendations |

PubMed and the Cochrane Library provide databases of peer-reviewed medical research. Reviewers use these databases to confirm cited studies actually support the published text. Publish the article if it passes the final safety check. Reviewer names and credentials appear at the top and bottom of every article alongside the review date. This transparency allows readers to verify the qualifications of the specific medical review board member. Read our detailed [methodology](https://fascinatingdentistry.com/methodology/) for further information regarding our editorial standards. 

## How Do We Verify AHPRA Registrations and Credentials?

We verify credentials by **checking every reviewer's AHPRA registration quarterly and requiring conflict of interest disclosures**. All reviewers must hold current Australian registration. The Australian Health Practitioner Regulation Agency maintains the public record of registered health practitioners. Every three months, our administration team queries the AHPRA database using each reviewer's profile.

AHPRA registration confirms a dentist holds the legal right to practise dentistry in Australia. Reviewers must maintain unrestricted general or specialist registration. Unrestricted registration indicates no limitations on a practitioner's ability to provide care. We mandate strict conflict of interest disclosures. Reviewers complete formal documentation disclosing any clinic ownership, product endorsements, or financial relationships with dental manufacturers. 

Reviewers do not review content related to their own commercial products or dental clinics. A reviewer paid by a specific dental implant manufacturer cannot review articles mentioning that specific brand. This policy ensures unbiased, bipartisan information delivery. Readers receive factual information free from commercial promotion. Access our [editorial policy](https://fascinatingdentistry.com/editorial-policy/) for information regarding our governance structure.

## What Are Common Questions About Our Team?

### Who reviews the dental content on Fascinating Dentistry?

All clinical content is reviewed by **AHPRA-registered dentists with clinical and academic experience**. Our reviewers include specialists like prosthodontists, oral surgeons, and general practitioners with advanced training.

### How do I know the reviewers are qualified?

Every reviewer holds **current AHPRA registration, which we verify quarterly**. Reviewer credentials, including qualifications and registration numbers, appear on their bio pages and on every article they review.

### Can I become a medical reviewer for Fascinating Dentistry?

We are expanding our review team. If you are **an AHPRA-registered dentist with clinical experience** and interest in evidence-based dentistry, please [contact us](https://fascinatingdentistry.com/contact/) through our contact page.

### Do reviewers have any conflicts of interest?

All reviewers must disclose **conflicts of interest, including clinic ownership or commercial relationships**. Reviewers do not review content related to their own practices or commercial interests.
